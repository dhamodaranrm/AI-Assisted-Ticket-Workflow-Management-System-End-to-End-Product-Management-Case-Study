# AI Ticket Management System

## Problem Statement
Manual ticket categorization and SLA tracking consume time and create delays.

## Objective
Build an AI-assisted ticket workflow that categorizes incidents, predicts SLA breaches, and improves operational efficiency.

## Features
- AI-based ticket categorization
- SLA prediction
- Priority recommendation
- Dashboard reporting
- Workflow automation

## Stakeholders
- Support Teams
- Product Teams
- Operations
- Management

## Success Metrics
- Faster ticket resolution
- Reduced SLA breaches
- Improved reporting visibility
