**AI Use Case Template**

**Overview**

The AI Use Case Template provides a standardized framework for documenting, evaluating, and prioritizing Artificial Intelligence opportunities within the AI-Assisted Ticket Workflow Management System.

As organizations adopt AI-driven capabilities, it becomes essential to consistently capture business challenges, AI solution approaches, expected outcomes, risks, and success metrics. This template ensures all AI initiatives are assessed using a common structure before development and implementation.

**Purpose**

The purpose of this template is to:

Standardize AI use case documentation across teams

Align AI initiatives with business objectives

Identify automation and optimization opportunities

Evaluate feasibility, complexity, and expected business value

Support prioritization during roadmap planning

Facilitate stakeholder reviews and approval processes



**When to Use This Template:**

Use this template whenever a new AI capability, automation opportunity, or intelligent workflow enhancement is identified, including:

Ticket categorization and classification

Priority prediction

Automated ticket routing

SLA breach prediction

Root cause analysis

Agent assistance and recommendations

Knowledge article generation

Customer sentiment analysis

Predictive workload management

AI-powered reporting and analytics

**Benefits**

Using a structured AI Use Case Template enables teams to:

Reduce ambiguity during AI initiative planning

Improve decision-making and prioritization

Create repeatable evaluation processes

Increase stakeholder alignment

Accelerate AI product development

Improve governance and compliance readiness

**Expected Outcome**

Every approved AI use case should provide a clear understanding of:

The business problem being solved

The AI capability being introduced

Expected operational and business impact

Success criteria and measurement approach

Associated risks and mitigation strategies

This ensures AI investments remain aligned with organizational goals while delivering measurable value to users and stakeholders.
