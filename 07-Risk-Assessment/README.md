
**Risk Assessment**

**Overview**

This folder contains the risk assessment artifacts for the AI-Assisted Ticket Workflow Management System.

The objective is to identify, evaluate, and mitigate potential risks that could impact project delivery, system performance, AI accuracy, security, compliance, user adoption, and operational effectiveness.

**Contents**
**Risk Assessment Matrix** - Identifies and evaluates project, technical, operational, and AI-related risks.
**Risk Register Template** - Tracks risks, impact, probability, mitigation actions, ownership, and status throughout the project lifecycle.


**Risk Categories**

The assessment covers:

-Project and delivery risks

-Technical and integration risks

-Data quality risks

-AI model and prediction risks

-Security and compliance risks

-Operational and user adoption risks

**Objectives**

-Proactively identify potential issues

-Reduce delivery and operational risks

-Improve decision-making and governance

-Support successful implementation and adoption

-Ensure continuous monitoring and mitigation

**Outcome**

A structured risk management approach helps ensure that the solution remains reliable, secure, scalable, and aligned with business objectives throughout its lifecycle.
