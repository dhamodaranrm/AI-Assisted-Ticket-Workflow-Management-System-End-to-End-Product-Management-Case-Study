
**AI Model Monitoring**

**Overview**

This folder contains artifacts used to monitor the performance, reliability, and effectiveness of AI capabilities within the AI-Assisted Ticket Workflow Management System.

Continuous monitoring helps ensure that AI models remain accurate, relevant, and aligned with business objectives after deployment.

**Contents**

AI Model Monitoring Document - Defines monitoring objectives, metrics, thresholds, and governance processes.

AI Model Health Scorecard - Tracks key AI performance indicators and identifies potential degradation or retraining needs.

**Monitoring Areas**

-Model Accuracy

-Prediction Performance

-Data Quality

-Model Drift

-User Adoption

-Recommendation Effectiveness

-SLA Prediction Reliability


**Objectives**

-Detect performance degradation early

-Monitor AI effectiveness over time

-Improve user trust and adoption

-Support continuous model improvement

-Ensure responsible AI governance

**Outcome**

A structured monitoring approach ensures that AI capabilities continue delivering reliable, measurable, and business-aligned outcomes throughout their lifecycle.
