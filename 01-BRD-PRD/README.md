Overview

This repository contains the Business Requirement Document (BRD) and Product Requirement Document (PRD) for the AI Ticket Workflow Management System project. The documentation outlines the business objectives, functional requirements, workflows, product vision, and implementation approach for building an AI-assisted ticket management solution.

The project focuses on improving operational efficiency through intelligent ticket categorization, SLA prediction, priority recommendations, workflow automation, and centralized reporting.

Purpose:


The primary objective of this repository is to maintain structured and well-documented business and product requirements that serve as a reference for

-Business stakeholders

-Product Managers

-Developers

-QA teams

-Operations teams

-Project Management teams

This repository helps ensure alignment between business goals, technical implementation, and delivery expectations throughout the project lifecycle.




Project Scope

The AI Ticket Workflow Management System is designed to:

-Automate ticket categorization using AI/ML

-Predict SLA breaches proactively

-Recommend ticket priorities

-Improve incident handling workflows

-Enhance operational visibility through dashboards and reporting

-Reduce manual effort and response delays
